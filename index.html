A virtual lecture hall is an online platform for lectures.

The program consists of a virtual hall, where the lecture is projected onto the screen in video format,
and 3D panoramas that create the effect of presence.

Virtual lessons

first person

The first-person view and the feeling of being in the painted world is one of the main features of virtual reality. This allows you to conduct lessons entirely in virtual reality.

Ultra focusing

Allows you to concentrate

The virtual world that surrounds the viewer on all sides at 360 degrees allows you to fully concentrate on the material and not be distracted by external stimuli.

Involvement

Forever and ever

Virtual reality allows you to change scenarios, influence the course of an experiment or solve a mathematical problem in a playful and understandable way. During the virtual lesson, you can see the world of the past through the eyes of a historical character, go on a journey through the human body in a microcapsule or choose the right course on a ship.

How does it work?

Lectures are held online, and users can connect from anywhere in the world. The listener has the opportunity in real time to interact with the lecturer, ask clarifying questions and hear questions from other users. The work of the listener's microphones is controlled by a lecturer.

3D panoramas are preloaded onto the user's phone before the lecture begins, this allows you to reduce Internet traffic and avoid technical problems during operation. The lecturer controls the course of lectures and moves users from the lecture hall to 3D panoramas.
